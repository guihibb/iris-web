Find out what the Iris project is and aim at

Iris is the leap in education you've been waiting for.

We are taking education to the next level by providing students with educational tools they can use at home, in the classroom and beyond.
The Iris project is designed as a proactive interpersonal relationship between the artificial intelligence and the user. The objective of the Iris project is to support all stages of human-computer interaction by integrating knowledge sources from various technologies, such as information retrieval and reasoning, natural language processing, machine learning.

Iris revolutionizes education. Iris creates a space where students are automatically created with tools to help them learn and achieve their goals, while encouraging them to actively participate in the learning process. With this tool, you can use your computer as a teaching assistant, providing feedback and guidance as you learn.
